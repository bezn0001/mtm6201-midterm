About

At the heart of Ottawa, nestled in a cozy corner of the city, lies a hidden gem that delights the senses and warms the soul - Deliziosa, your small, local bakery. With a passion for crafting exceptional baked goods, we have been serving the Ottawa community with love and dedication for over a decade.

We believe in the power of local ingredients, and we source as many of our supplies as possible from Ottawa's very own farmers and producers. It's our way of supporting the community that has embraced us so warmly. Deliziosa's offerings draw from the rich tapestry of global cuisine, ensuring you get a taste of the world right here in Ottawa.
